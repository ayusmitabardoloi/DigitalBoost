# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Project Name:** DigitalBoost
**Target Audience:** Businesses seeking digital marketing services.

## Overview
DigitalBoost is a modern, responsive landing page for a startup digital marketing agency. The website aims to showcase services, build trust through statistics and testimonials, and convert visitors into leads via a contact form and newsletter subscription.

## Core Features & Sections
1. **Responsive Navigation Bar**: Sticky header with logo and mobile hamburger menu.
2. **Hero Section**: High-impact headline, subheadline, and strong "Get Started" CTA.
3. **Services Section**: 6 distinct services (SEO, Social Media, Content, Email, PPC, Analytics).
4. **About Us Section**: Company intro with animated counters for key statistics (Happy Clients, Projects, Years Experience).
5. **Why Choose Us Section**: Value propositions displayed as feature cards with icons.
6. **Portfolio / Case Studies**: Showcase of 3 sample projects with progress bars showing growth metrics.
7. **Testimonials**: Interactive slider showing 3 customer reviews.
8. **FAQ Accordion**: Expandable frequently asked questions section.
9. **Contact & Newsletter**: Contact form with client-side validation and newsletter sign-up.
10. **Footer**: Social media links and copyright information.

## Design Requirements
- **Color Palette**: Blue, purple, and white with modern gradients.
- **Typography**: Clean, professional sans-serif typography.
- **Aesthetics**: Rounded cards, subtle drop shadows, glassmorphism hints.
- **Animations**: Hover effects on interactive elements, fade-ins on scroll, light loading screen.
- **Layout**: Mobile-first responsive design, ensuring perfect display across all devices.
